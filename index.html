What
Highly contagious illness in the AO
Where
Estonia, Latvia, Lithuania, Poland
Symptoms
  • Coughing
  • Nausea/Vomiting/Diarrhea (N/V/D)
  • Headache
  • Serious fatigue
  • Rash* (Seek care immediately)

Prevention Measures
  • Regular hand washing
  • Wear mask when in shared spaces
  • Maintain distance from others when possible
  • Report symptoms promptly to medical staff

Quarantine Procedures

BALTIC FLU PROTOCOLS

Quarantine Protocol

WHO: Soldiers who have been in close contact with a confirmed case but are not showing symptoms

DURATION: FIVE (5) days from the last date of exposure

PROCEDURE: If symptoms develop during quarantine, begin isolation immediately

Isolation Protocol

WHO: Soldiers who are showing symptoms of Baltic Flu

DURATION: Minimum of SEVEN (7) days after symptom onset

REQUIREMENTS TO END ISOLATION:

  • Resolution of fever for at least 24 hours
  • Improvement of other symptoms
  • Medical clearance required
